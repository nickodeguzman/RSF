Core Values

    INTEGRITY- We aim to be truthful, honest, accountable, and trustworthy in all aspects of life.

    COMPASSION- We respond with love, kindness, and respect to every individual, group or community.

    TEAMWORK- We work and win together as one team.

    STEWARDSHIP- We support and preserve benefactor intent across generation to be financially responsible.

    HUMILITY- We strive to develop a sprint of gratefulness and servanthood in all of our relationship .

    EXCELLENCE- We will deliver the best and highest quality of service that will exceed the expectation of our benefactors, individual and the community in everything we do.

    HUMAN DIGNITY- We believe that all men are created in the image of God(imago dei) and have been created with a purpose that should be treated with respect and equality.

    GOD FEARING- We acknowledge God as the Creator of all and we honor and submit to his will.
